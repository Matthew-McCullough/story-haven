# � Once Upon A Time...

A magical web application providing enchanted bedtime stories for families everywhere. Once Upon A Time offers a curated collection of public domain stories with advanced filtering options and text-to-speech functionality to help parents create truly magical bedtime experiences for their children.

## ✨ Features

- **Story Filtering**: Filter by genre, time period, age group, and character gender preferences
- **Text-to-Speech**: Built-in narration with male/female voice options, adjustable speed and pitch
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on all devices
- **Free Content**: All stories sourced from public domain collections
- **Accessibility Focused**: Designed for families with varying technical abilities

## 🎭 Story Collection

Our collection includes:
- Fairy Tales & Fables
- Fantasy Adventures
- Science Fiction
- Rhyming Stories
- Biblical Stories
- Picture Books
- Chapter Books

Stories span from biblical times to modern day, with content appropriate for ages 2-12+.

Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000) with your browser to see the result.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Framework**: Next.js 15 with TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Audio**: Web Speech API for text-to-speech
- **Linting**: ESLint with Next.js configuration

## 💝 Mission

Once Upon A Time was inspired by the work of volunteers who read to low-income children through churches and community organizations. Our goal is to extend that same love and care to families who have internet access but may not have physical access to libraries or bookstores, creating a digital castle of stories for all.
